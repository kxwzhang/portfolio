Why Software Engineering

I am a software engineer with a B.S. in Statistical Data Science. Engineering was always something I enjoyed, whether it was building Legos when I was younger or constructing statistical models to perform predictive analysis.

While working through my college degree, I realize that a lot of my work could be automated. This sparked my interest in programming which led to my passion for designing user-facing products in software engineering using some of my favorite frameworks like React / Redux.

When I am not developing, I like to spend my time reading books (I highly recommend Atlas Shrugged by Aynn Rand), exercising, or playing video games.
